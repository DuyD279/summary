About

A social platform where people control their own preferences without limiting freedom of speech and overall quality.

Expectations from the team

Mindset

Self-motivated. This whole thing could be a long term commitment without any results, this requires each and every founder to be able to work through the process of weekly workloads and meetings.

Workload

Every week there will be a meeting (usually on sunday) where we discuss overall progress, come up with new ideas as well as refining the app as a whole.

Deadlines

There will be a deadline for each phase of the project, these are not hard deadlines as problems could occur. But overall, it is expected to meet these deadlines.

Communication and Trust

This project would require each member to communicate their questions as well as any internal conflicts in order for the whole thing to work. It is expected of us to not be selfish, ideas of each individual will always be tagged with that member. Member will evaluate each other in order to divide percentages
